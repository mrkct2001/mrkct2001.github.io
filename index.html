Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this Retreat Right for ME?

This retreat is designed especially for women who are interested in travel, adventure and self-reflection. The themes we’ll explore during this retreat are wellness, setting goals for a positive future, and moving beyond our comfort zones by taking the steps on our individual journeys to live the lives we’ve imagined.

The setting for this trip lush and tropical, nestled on a private, beautiful resort in the Caribbean. This is an ideal setting for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Can I come alone?

Yes, we welcome you to come alone or with a friend! You will be in great company and will have many opportunities to connect with others in a supportive, fun and relaxing environment.

What can I expect to gain from this retreat?

We expect all guests to be open to a unique adventure, which will help them to reflect and gain clarity into their motivations and goals. We wish to guide you to connect with others, grow and take action on infusing more meaning and purpose into daily living.

Who will be there? How many people will be there?

Those who join us on this trip will be 10-12 women who are ready to step up to the challenge of trying something new. These women wish to learn, grow and experience adventures among the culture and key landmarks of the Riviera Maya in Mexico.

What are the retreat leaders like?

We are fun and adventurous foodies, who have been traveling, planning, and visioning together for several years. We are inspired to explore the connections of femininity, health, culture and travel. We invite you to become our traveling companions!

Amy Lee loves all things techie, is a graphic designer and has a passion for all things creative, maps, yoga and design. She has a background in international business, fine arts and business administration.

Nikki Jay loves all things fashion, is a Certified Health Education Specialist, jewelry designer and Latin dance instructor. She has a background in domestic violence counseling, women’s studies, psychology, biology and health promotion and education.

What makes this retreat unique?

We have planned quite a few activities for a great time connecting with one another.

While you are exploring the retreat, we will focus on you!

This retreat is unique because of the private, tropical setting and the workshops and activities provided by Sisters with Passports. You will be able to focus on yourself and make plans for your future, but also have fun and connect with others in a relaxing setting. We will visit historic ruins in Coba, do yoga on the beach, swim in a cenote and participate in team-building adventures at Xplor.

In what time zone will we stay during the retreat?

The entire Yucatan Peninsula is within the Eastern Standard Time Zone.
