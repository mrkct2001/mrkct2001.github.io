The Event

Don'T Delay Join us

Join us for exploration, adventure and self-reflection on the warm, sunny coast of the Yucatan Peninsula, cradled in the Caribbean Sea. Be inspired to live your best year yet by challenging yourself, building connections, and maximizing your well-being.

We invite you on this exclusive quest to reveal what is true for you. Reach the places outside of your comfort zone as we guide you to the possibilities and next steps on your path.

  • Our Mission - Nurture your spirit
  • Our Goal - Inspire fearlessness
  • Desired Outcome - Challenge you to action

Meet our team

Over the past six years, two best friends Amy Lee and Nikki Jay, have dedicated one week at the dawn of each new year to reflect on the previous year, illuminate shadows in the present, and set actions in motion for the future.

Amy Lee

Nikki Jay

We hold the mirror to each other, asking; Who am I now? and Who am I willing to be next year? How many times do we tell ourselves; No, Later, and Not now? Give yourself permission to say Yes!, Now! and I'm ready!

Taninah

featured on HGTV and The Travel Channel, is ten acres of the most unique vacation experience and exotic property in the Riviera Maya

COBA

(pronounced cō-bǝ) is an ancient Maya city. The site is the nexus of the largest network of stone causeways of the ancient Mayan world

Xplor

Xplor park contains zip-lines, amphibious vehicles, rafts, swimming in underground rivers, spelunking and hammock splash, these activities take place in natural settings outdoors and within underground environments.

XPU HA'

(pronounced Shpoo-Ha) is a pristine, virtually untouched stretch of silken white sand and turquoise waters of the Riviera Maya.
