Suite accommodations

  • La Casa - Round, quite charming two floor guest house with a covered wrap-around outdoor terrace. (Quad suite)
  •  
  • La Quinta - Locally milled hardwoods, in this walled structure with wooden shutter panels and framed with mosquito screens. (Quad suite)
  •  
  • El Bungalow - Tropical and breezy structure with wooden shutter panels and framed with mosquito screens. (Double suite) this suite is fully booked.
  •  
  • La Cabana - Enchanting raised platform palapa that evokes a sense of a tropical island dwelling. Ideal for those who want single-level access. (Single or double suite)

Taninah Resort - Located just an hour and fifteen minutes south of Cancun International Airport, Taninah is a fully staffed and managed, meals-included, ecologically friendly private jungle retreat offering an exciting alternative to a standard and sterile vacation. This unrivaled, one-of-a-kind vacation property has been featured on HGTV's Extreme Homes, The Travel Channel's Amazing Vacation Homes, in the magazine Caribbean World, and in the Spring 2008 issue of Travel + Leisure, Family Edition. No other vacation rental property in the Riviera Maya has been so honored or recognized.

Transportation Information - Flights are not included. We estimate flight pricing from the U.S. to average around $550. Check nearby airports for best prices. If you are interested in working with a travel agency to book your flight, we can connect you with a contact at AAA Travel. You will arrive at the Cancun International Airport in Cancun, Mexico and then finish your trip to Tulum by shuttle. Airport transfer shuttles are included in your retreat fee. Shuttles will be provided during arrival and departure days of the retreat. If you cannot make the designated shuttle times for any reason, we can arrange individual transportation at an additional fee of $100 one way ($200 round trip).
