Tulum Retreat - January 12-17, 2017

Join us at the Taninah Resort

Frequently asked questions (FAQ's):

  • Is this Retreat Right for You?
    • This retreat is designed especially for women who are interested in travel, adventure and self-reflection. The themes we’ll explore during this retreat are wellness, setting goals for a positive future, and moving beyond our comfort zones by taking the steps on our individual journeys to live the lives we’ve imagined.
    • The setting for this trip lush and tropical, nestled on a private, beautiful resort in the Caribbean. An ideal setting for relaxation and rejuvenation.
  • Can I come alone?
    • Yes, we welcome you to come alone or with a friend! You will be in great company and will have many opportunities to connect with others in a supportive, fun and relaxing environment.
  • What can I expect to gain from this retreat?
    • We expect all guests to be open to a unique adventure, which will help them to reflect, gain clarity into their motivations and goals. We wish to guide you to connect with others, grow and take action on infusing more meaning and purpose into daily living.
    • * Contact us if your questions were not answered here or if you need additional information. See you soon!
