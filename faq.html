Frequently Asked Questions

Join us at the Taninah Resort for the Tulum retreat - January 12-17, 2017

Is this retreat right for me?
  • Why should I go on this retreat?
    • This retreat is designed especially for women who are interested in travel, adventure and self-reflection. The themes we’ll explore during this retreat are wellness, setting goals for a positive future, and moving beyond our comfort zones by taking the steps on our individual journeys to live the lives we’ve imagined.
    • The setting for this trip lush and tropical, nestled on a private, beautiful resort in the Caribbean. This is an ideal setting for relaxation and rejuvenation.
  • Can I come alone?
    • Yes, we welcome you to come alone or with a friend! You will be in great company and will have many opportunities to connect with others in a supportive, fun and relaxing environment.
  • What can I expect to gain from this retreat?
    • We expect all guests to be open to a unique adventure, which will help them to reflect and gain clarity into their motivations and goals. We wish to guide you to connect with others, grow and take action on infusing more meaning and purpose into daily living.
  • Who will be there? / How many people will be there?
    • Those who join us on this trip will be 10-12 women who are ready to step up to the challenge of trying something new. These women wish to learn, grow and experience adventures among the culture and key landmarks of the Riviera Maya in Mexico.
  • What are the retreat leaders like?
    • We are fun and adventurous foodies, who have been traveling, planning, and visioning together for several years. We are inspired to explore the connections of femininity, health, culture and travel. We invite you to become our traveling companions!
    • Amy Lee loves all things techie, is a graphic designer and has a passion for all things creative, maps, yoga and design. She has a background in international business, fine arts and business administration.
    • Nikki Jay loves all things fashion, is a Certified Health Education Specialist, jewelry designer and Latin dance instructor. She has a background in domestic violence counseling, women’s studies, psychology, biology and health promotion and education.
  • What makes this retreat unique?
    • We have planned quite a few activities for a great time connecting with one another. While you are exploring the retreat, we will focus on you!
    • This retreat is unique because of the private, tropical setting and the workshops and activities provided by Sisters with Passports. This is an ideal setting for relaxation and rejuvenation.
    • You will be able to focus on yourself and make plans for your future, but also have fun and connect with others in a relaxing setting. We will visit historic ruins in Coba, do yoga on the beach, swim in a cenote and participate in team-building adventures at Xplor.

    What about Registration, Deposit and Costs?
    • How long is the retreat?
      • The retreat will be held over six days and five nights.
    • What amenities are available at the resort?
      • Please see this link from Taninah for a detailed list of amenities: http://www.taninah.com/amenities.php
    • Can I have my own room?
      • Due to the unique features of this retreat, most guests will share a two-bed, two-person room. If you are interested in booking a single room, please be prepared to book this option soon as accommodations are first come, first served.
      • Please visit the “Register” page to review and book accommodations.
    • When should I book this trip?
      • If you would like to have one of the discounted early bird packages, you will need to submit your deposit by July 15, 2016. If you choose the regular payment option, or the pay in full option, your deposit or full payment will be due on August 1, 2016.
      • If you choose a payment plan (not the pay in full option), your first payment is due on September 1, 2016 and your final payment is due on November 1, 2016.
      • Please see the “Register” page for payment schedules and costs.
      • We expect that this retreat will sell out; don’t delay!
    • How much does the retreat cost and when will I need to submit payment?
      • The price of the retreat is $1,475. However, there we provide three payment options that can offer you a substantial savings (up to $150). You are able to choose the best option to suit your needs. See the “Register” page for a breakdown of pricing and payment deadlines for each option.
    • What is included in the retreat fee?
      • We are staying at an all-inclusive resort. Your fees cover:
        • Includes gratuity for on-site staff and a concierge who are present 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
        • Exclusive use of the 10-acre, private property (see map: http://www.taninah.com/property-map.php)
        • Chef-prepared meals - daily breakfast, lunch, and dinner. These meals will be provided daily while you are at Taninah; however, on Day 3 you will have the opportunity to explore offsite. If you choose to venture out of the resort area to explore, you will need to bring additional money to purchase off-site meals, souvenirs, and other items and services (such as massage).
        • Sisters with Passports workshops and materials
        • Airport transfer to Taninah * (round trip). Please refer to the “How will I get from the airport and from the retreat space?” question for additional information about ground transportation parameters.
        • Round trip transportation to Xplor
        • Entrance to Xplor
        • Round trip transportation to Coba
        • Entrance to Coba
        • Round trip transportation to the cenote
        • Round trip transportation to Xpu Ha’ Beach
        • Sunrise Yoga
        • Lodging (your choice of single, double or quad). Accommodation options are first reserved (by deposit), first served.
        • Please visit the “Register” page for additional information.
    • How do I reserve my spot at the retreat?
      • Visit the Sisters with Passports website and select the “Register” page. Choose which payment option works best for you and submit your payment to book your spot. We expect this trip to book quickly, so register as soon as possible.

      What about Lodging, Travel and Transportation?
      • Where will I stay?
        • We will stay at Taninah Resort, which is located in Mexico’s Riviera Maya, just one hour and 15 minutes south of the Cancun International Airport. This unique vacation property has been featured as a must-visit travel destination in HGTV’s Extreme Homes, The Travel Channel’s Amazing Vacation Homes, in the Magazine Caribbean World, and in the Spring 2008 issue of Travel + Leisure, Family Edition. For additional information about Taninah, visit http://www.taninah.com/home.php.
      • Will I need my passport?
        • Yes. Because we will travel outside the U.S., each guest will need to have a valid passport to come on this trip. We recommend reviewing the United States Postal Services guidelines and timelines for obtaining your passport: https://www.usps.com/international/passports.htm. If you need a new or renewed passport, please do not delay applying for this. Note: we encourage all guests who will come on this trip to have their passports in hand by September 2016.
      • Will I need to secure my own flight?
        • Yes. Travel to Mexico (flight) is not included in the cost of your retreat, so you must reserve and purchase your own round-trip flight. We encourage you to do this as soon as possible after booking your spot on the retreat.
      • When should I book my flight to arrive at the Cancun International Airport?
        • We've booked two shuttles for our groups arrivals. One at noon and another at 6:pm EST. Please plan to book your arrival flight to Cancun International Airport (CUN) on Thursday, January 11, 2017 with enough time to secure your luggage and make the shuttle.
        • We've also booked two shuttles for our groups departures. One at 7:am EST. and another at noon. Please plan to book your departure flight from Cancun International Airport (CUN) on Thursday, January 17, 2017 accordingly. Note: Taninah is approximately 1 - 1.5 hours from CUN.
      • how will I get to and from the airport and Taninah Retreat?
        • Ground transportation is included in your retreat fee. If you are unable to make these times, individual transportation can be arranged for approximately $99. each way, at your expense.
      • How will we get around once we’re in Mexico?
        • Transportation to retreat activity locations (Xpu Ha’ Beach, Coba and Xplor) is included in the retreat cost. If other transportation needs arise, please let us know as we can work with the retreat space to help coordinate the travel; these transportation costs will be additional to you and will not be covered in the retreat fee.

                Insurance?
                • Is travel insurance recommended?
                  • Yes, travel insurance is highly recommended and is not covered in the cost of your retreat. We encourage every guest to purchase travel insurance (to provide protection for protect travel arrangement/flight, medical, baggage protection and worldwide emergency assistance). Sisters with Passports will not be held responsible for servicing and/or refunding reservations affected by acts of God, terrorism, wildfires, accidental fire, lightning strike, or any other major cause outside of Sisters with Passports’’ immediate control. For this reason, we request that guests obtain travel insurance through a reputable travel insurance provider. Failure to obtain adequate travel insurance could result in total loss of reservation monies paid to date if we are unable to host said retreat due to one of the causes listed above.
